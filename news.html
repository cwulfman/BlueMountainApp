New Project Page

We are happy to announce the launch of the new Blue Mountain project page: http://bluemountain.princeton.edu. Developed to better meet the needs of researchers and to prepare for a large-scale expansion of services over the next year, the new project page provides a browsable catalog of Blue Mountain contents with links to full-text versions in the Blue Mountain Archive. This semester, Sophie Setia (PhD candidate in English, University of London) will help us compile bibliographic histories for Mask, 291, Coeur a barbe, Surrealisme, SIC, Poesia, and Veshch. That rich data will be integrated into the new project page and allow for even more powerful discovery of Blue Mountain content.
